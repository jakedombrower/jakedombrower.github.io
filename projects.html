The Harbinger Online

The official website of the Harborfields High School Tornado News Team. Here you can view a variety of information such as daily announcements and school events. Also, you can read some of the news articles published by the News Team or even download your own digital copy of The Harbinger, the team's print edition newspaper.

Urban Specialty Woods

Urban Specialty Woods is a speciality lumber yard, carrying one of a kind material. Built off of Ruby on Rails, this website guarentees an incredibly intuitive interface for both the end-user, and the warehouse owner. Included on this site is a photo gallery, and an extensive catalogue of all the business has to offer.
