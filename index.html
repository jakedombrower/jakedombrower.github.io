MY BIO

Programming. Some hide in fear when they hear about it while some choose to run right into it. I'm the latter of the two. For as long as I can remember I have been interested in technology. In elementary school, I messed around with Microsoft Batch. In middle school I took on BASIC. However, by the time high school rolled around, things got real.

Freshman year I set my sights on Rails. Although challenging at first, practice made (almost) perfect and Rails became my specialty. If somebody needs a website, I'm their guy.

Well, here I am. One year and a plethora of projects later. My typical day consists of three things: Food, Sleep, and Programming. What started as a passion, eventually became my life.
